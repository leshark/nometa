Please do not ask meta-questions!

Meta-question — question which assumes follow-up quesitons, for example:

May I ask a question?
Is there anyone doing X? Есть, кто разбирается в N?
Has anyone done the ML course on Coursera? I have a question about neural networks…
Guys, got a question about %framework_name%

— «Yeah, sure, but what's wrong with these questions and why shouldn't I ask them?»

— They waste time! Both yours and other people who are trying to help you! You're trying to be polite starting with "small talk" like in face-to-face meeting. But Internet is different. People are typing much slower than they speak. Instead of being polite you just make the other person wait while you formulate the question, and this leads to performance degradation.

The same applies to messages like: «Greetings», «Hi, are you here?», «Got a minute?», «Ping». Just ask a question!

Not to mention, if you're asking quesiton directly, it's open for asyncronyous communication. If the person is not available and you go offline before they come back, they can still answer the quesiton istead of looking on your «Hello» and thinking what happened.
